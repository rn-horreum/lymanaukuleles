COMING SOON

Pueo Ukuleles is a branch off idea of Lymana Ukuleles. Essentially Pueo Ukuleles are ukuleles made out of my spare wood in the factory. I do not want my wood to go to waste, so why not make some ukuleles out of it? Pueo Ukuleles are different from my main customs because I won't be putting as much attention into them. They will still have the same level of craftsmanship, but just at not at as high of a level. Therefore, these will be cheaper ukuleles. If you are a casual player or want to get something for your young nephew that won't have to worry about banging it up, please consider a Pueo Ukulele!
