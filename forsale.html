I was inspired to build BILL TAPIA'S RED UKULELE because he always wore red clothing and I thought a red ukulele would match his red clothes. His white ukulele was built because I saw a picture of him holding a white Kamaka pineapple ukulele, so I built him the white tenor. I miss Bill he had some great stories of old Hawaii.
