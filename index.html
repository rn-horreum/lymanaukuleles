ABOUT ME

Aloha, my name is Lyman Ashikawa and I was born and raised on the Hawaiian island of Oahu. I have been building ukuleles for the last 30 years and have since perfected my craft. I have not only made dozens of custom ukuleles for everyday people, but have also had the honor of building instruments for icons such as Bill Tapia (NOTE : could link to Bill Tapia story/page here). I hope you enjoy browsing my site and consider a Lymana Ukulele. I would be honored to craft you one!
