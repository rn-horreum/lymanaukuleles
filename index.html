ABOUT ME

Aloha, my name is Lyman Ashikawa and I was born and raised on the Hawaiian island of Oahu. I have been building ukuleles for the last 30 years and have since perfected my craft. I have not only made dozens of custom ukuleles for everyday people, but have also had the honor of building instruments for icons such as Bill Tapia (click the link to see the project).
